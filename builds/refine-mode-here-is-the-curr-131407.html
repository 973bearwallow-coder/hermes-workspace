About Jane

Jane is a lifelong Falls Church resident and dog lover. She started Paw Prints to give neighbors a warm, trustworthy option for daily dog walking and care. Your pup gets personal attention every step of the way. She loves dog-walking because it lets her share the simple joy of a wagging tail and a sunny morning with dogs who feel like family. Having grown up on the very streets she now walks, Jane cherishes the chance to keep her neighborhood connected one leash at a time.
