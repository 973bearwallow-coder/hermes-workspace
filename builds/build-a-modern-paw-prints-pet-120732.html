Meet Jane

Hi! I’m Jane, a lifelong Falls Church resident and certified dog lover. After years of volunteering at the local shelter, I started Paw Prints to give neighborhood dogs the same warmth my own pups get at home.

Every walk is leash-positive, stress-free, and full of sniffing rights. Your dog’s safety and happiness are my whole business.
