# Paw Prints Website & Business Strategy Notes
_Coached from AI Profit Boardroom calls, research, and Tom's direct input_

## SEO Strategy (from June 4 coaching call)
- **The combo that works for local businesses**: Local SEO + AEO (Agentic Engine Optimization) + Google Maps Pack ranking
- 50% of traffic now comes from AI platforms (ChatGPT, Gemini, etc.) scraping sites and making recommendations
- Focus on low-hanging local keyword opportunities vs competitors
- Do NOT buy generic SEO courses designed for companies targeting 500K+ visitors/day — Paw Prints is local
- Community connections and personal referrals outperform search rankings for local businesses
- SEO takes ~2 years minimum even with AI — but AEO + Maps Pack can accelerate results

## Lead Generation (from June 4 coaching call)
- **Rule #1**: Collect name/email FIRST, give value, THEN ask for commitment
- Never put an NDA or signup form between you and a potential lead
- "Path of least resistance" — every extra click loses people
- If you need an NDA for sensitive info, present the opportunity first, collect contact details, THEN do NDA before revealing details

## Notion as Central Hub (from Stephen's demo, June 4)
- Notion database as single source of truth + Notion Workers connected to Claude/Codex
- Auto-routes submissions to correct client/database based on content
- Webhook triggers: when submission marked "new," worker auto-queries RAG database and delivers formatted results
- Works from phone via voice — "I'm doing XYZ, document it and route it"
- After 1.5 years, AI learned db structure and can auto-create databases with proper tags
- **Potential upgrade path for Paw Prints**: Replace Telegram+cron workflow with Notion Workers
